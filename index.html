Protecting and Advocating for Animals: Standing with the Philippine Animal Welfare Society (PAWS)

Why should you support the PAWS?

  • Rescue and Rehabilitation: PAWS is dedicated to rescuing animals from neglect, abuse, and dangerous situations. They provide immediate care, medical treatment, and rehabilitation for rescued animals, ensuring their physical and emotional well-being. PAWS operates a shelter where these animals receive love, attention, and a chance for a better life.
  • Education and Advocacy: PAWS actively promotes animal welfare through education and advocacy programs. They conduct workshops, seminars, and campaigns to raise awareness about responsible pet ownership, animal rights, and the importance of compassion towards animals. By educating the public, PAWS seeks to foster a culture of empathy and kindness towards animals.
  • Legislative Initiatives: PAWS actively engages in lobbying and pushing for stronger animal welfare legislation in the Philippines. They work with lawmakers and government agencies to enact and enforce laws that protect animals from cruelty and ensure their welfare. PAWS' advocacy efforts have resulted in significant progress in animal protection laws, making a lasting impact on animal welfare in the country.
